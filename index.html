About

I am an H. C. Wang Assistant Professor at Cornell University. My research work is in the field of algebraic number theory, with a particular interest in Galois theory and deformations of Galois representations.

I received my Ph.D. from the University of Chicago under the guidance of Professor Frank Calegari. Before that, I completed my MMathCompSci degree at the University of Oxford in 2018, writing my dissertation under the supervision of Professor Minhyong Kim. Here is my CV.
