About

I am an H. C. Wang Assistant Professor at Cornell University. My research work is in the field of algebraic number theory, with a particular interest in Galois theory and deformations of Galois representations.

I completed my Ph.D. in June 2024 at the University of Chicago under the supervision of Professor Frank Calegari. Here is my CV.
